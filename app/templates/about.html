About Todo App

Our Mission

At Todo App, our mission is to help people stay organized and boost their productivity. We believe that a simple, well-designed todo application can make a significant difference in how people manage their tasks and achieve their goals.

We've created a clean, intuitive interface that makes it easy to create, organize, and track your tasks. With secure user accounts and cloud storage, your todos are always available wherever you go.

Our Story

Todo App was created by a team of developers who were frustrated with the complexity of existing task management solutions. We wanted something simple yet powerful, with just the right features to help people stay organized without overwhelming them.

After months of development and testing, we launched Todo App with a focus on user experience and simplicity. Since then, we've been continuously improving the application based on user feedback.

Our Technology

Todo App is built using modern web technologies to ensure a fast, responsive experience:

  • Flask - A lightweight Python web framework
  • Supabase - For authentication and database management
  • Bootstrap 5 - For responsive design
  • Lucide Icons - For beautiful, consistent iconography

We prioritize security and performance, ensuring that your data is protected and the application runs smoothly on all devices.
