About NewsletterChat

We're revolutionizing how people consume news by delivering personalized content directly to their favorite messaging platforms.

Founded in 2023, NewsletterChat was born from a simple observation: people spend most of their time in messaging apps, yet still have to switch to different platforms to get their news.

Our mission is to make staying informed effortless by bringing curated, relevant news directly to where you already are - your favorite chat applications.
